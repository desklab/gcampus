{% translate "Date and time" %}

{% translate "Location" %}
{% blocktrans trimmed %} Use a sketch to document the exact location of your measurement. Add significant objects located near to the water to make it easier to find the point of your measurement at a digital map later. You may also you use a mobile device to document the exact coordinates of your location. {% endblocktrans %}
{% translate "Environment" %}
{% blocktrans trimmed %} Note any relevant observation of the environment of the water. This may include the existing flora and fauna or the visual impression of the water quality. {% endblocktrans %}
{% translate "Parameter" %}
{% blocktrans trimmed %} Document the results (the Optical Density) of your chemical water analysis for each parameter. {% endblocktrans %}
